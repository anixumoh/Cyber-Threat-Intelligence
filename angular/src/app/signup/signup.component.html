Threat Compass is built from a customizable group of targeted modules.

This limited* trial version offers the following:

Credentials

Find actionable intelligence around leaked, stolen and sold user credentials. We locate them in real-time on the open, deep and dark web, along with information about relevant malware used to steal the information.

Credit Cards

Learn in real-time about stolen customer credit cards before they’re sold on the Dark Web, and block them regardless of how they were compromised.

Targeted Malware

Detect malware seeking to steal sensitive information or commit fraud. The aggressive solution proactively hunts down targeted malware and ‘Man in the Browser’ attacks, aimed specifically at your organization.

Hacktivism Operations

Monitor global hacktivism activity on social networks and the open and dark web that can affect your infrastructure. Using an advanced early-warning system and active geolocator, the module generates targeted threat intelligence to shield against potential attack vectors.
